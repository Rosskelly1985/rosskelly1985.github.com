About CCT Connect



CCT Connect is a socially interactive website made by the students of cct for the students of cct. It can be used by the students, once they register by using their student ids.
Its main functions are as follows:

(1) A chat system so they can interact with other students who are online,
(2) take a number of general knowledge computer quizes
(3) Use the online Library to access the latest videos and books on the subjects they are studying
(4) browse the entertainment section to keep up to date with College Events, as well as all latest music, movie and sports news"



My main responsibilities in this project were to design the over all layout of all the pages, as well as creating all the entertainment pages and adding all relevent information to each. I also designed all the headings and banners used throughout, wrote the groups weekley logs books and completed all the necessary documentation

    The "Boink me" mobile phone application is a 100% free mobile phone dating application. Users have to register, create a profile and turn there location on if they are looking to meet somebody.
    Once there location is on, they are visible on the gps system on the phone, which allows users in the srea to see whos near them and looking to hook up.
